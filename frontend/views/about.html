Quick Start

There are two main entry points:

Genres hierarchy

Open Genres section, find your favorite musical genre in the list, then click Learn More button. Proceed studying its subgenres, artists in the genre, or listen audio samples.

Direct Search

Type any name in the search field below the site title, e.g.: Deep Purple. In the drop-down list next to the search field select what are you searching for: Artist, Album or Track. Click Go! button. After a while you will be redirected to Search Results page. The best matches will be ath the top.

Tip: if you do not remember exact name, type its part. The search engine will do its best to find what you are looking for.
