Quick Start

There are two main entry points: via Genres and Direct Search.

Genres hierarchy

Open Genres section, find your favorite musical genre in the list, then click Learn More button. Proceed studying its subgenres, artists in the genre, or listen audio samples.

Tip: Many genres have their sub-genres as well as parent genres.

Direct Search

Type any name in the search field below the site title, e.g.: Deep Purple. In the drop-down list next to the search field select what are you searching for: Artist, Album or Track. Click Go button. After a while you will be redirected to Search Results page. The best matches will usually be at the top.

Tip: if you do not remember exact name, type its part. The search engine will do its best to find what you are looking for.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q:
Can I listen to full versions of audio tracks instead of short samples?
A:
Sorry, not yet. To do that, you have to be a Napster authorized user. For now, cross-site authorization is not implemented. I hope, it will be available in the next version. Stay in touch!
Q:
How do I find covers of my favourite song?
A:
Type the song name in the search box. Select "Track" in the drop-down list. Click Go button.
Q:
Is international content available?
A:
For now, only partially. We depend on Napster service, which is mostly English language oriented. In later versions, user will be able to choose her preffered language.
Q:
Does your site use cookies? Do you collect user information by any means?
A:
Absolutely, not! Maybe, in following versions we will introduce cookies for personalization.
